Travel Adapters for the Netherlands

The Netherlands uses Type F (Schuko) sockets. Here you'll find the adapters you need when traveling from the Netherlands to other countries.

Netherlands Standard (Type F)

The Netherlands uses Type F (Schuko) sockets with 230V and 50Hz. The socket has two round pins with two grounding clips on the sides.

Popular Destinations from the Netherlands

USA and Canada

For travels to the USA and Canada, you'll need a Type A/B adapter. These countries use 120V, so check if your devices support this voltage.
