Travel Adapters for the UK

The UK uses Type G sockets. Here you'll find the adapters you need when traveling from the UK to other countries.

UK Standard (Type G)

The UK uses Type G sockets with 230V and 50Hz. The socket has three rectangular pins in a triangular pattern with a fuse.

Popular Destinations from the UK

USA and Canada

For travels to the USA and Canada, you'll need a Type A/B adapter. These countries use 120V, so check if your devices support this voltage.
